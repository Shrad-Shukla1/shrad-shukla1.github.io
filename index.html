About Me

I am a large-scale hydrologist and Researcher at the Climate Hazards Center (CHC) at the University of California, Santa Barbara. My research focuses on improving drought monitoring and seasonal scale climate forecasting to support food security early warning systems globally.

Currently, I hold editorial roles for Hydrology and Earth System Sciences and Journal of Hydrometeorology.
