About Me

I am a Researcher at the Climate Hazards Center (CHC) at UC Santa Barbara. My research focuses on improving global drought monitoring and early warning capabilities using advanced Earth Observations, Land Surface Models, and seasonal climate forecasts.

I have secured over $25M in research funding as PI or co-PI from NASA, NOAA, USAID, and USGS. Currently, I lead the NASA & USAID SERVIR Applied Science Team for the "Weather and Climate" thematic area, facilitating coordination across global hubs.

Beyond research, I serve as Vice Chair for the AGU WaterSciCon and was recently selected as a Fulbright Specialist (2021-2025), where I collaborate with NGOs in India to enhance climate resilience for indigenous communities.
