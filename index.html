About Me

I am a Researcher at the Climate Hazards Center (CHC) at UC Santa Barbara. My research focuses on improving global drought monitoring and early warning capabilities using advanced Earth Observations, Land Surface Models, and subseasonal to seasonal climate forecasts.

I co-authored the book "Drought Early Warning and Forecasting: Theory and Practice" (Elsevier, 2020), which was a Finalist for the 2021 PROSE Awards in Earth Science.
